Carote Fermentate

how to lacto ferment delicious carrots easily at home

Ingredients

  • Fresh carrots
  • Tap water
  • Table salt

Steps

  1. wash the carrots under tap water
  2. put the carrots in a clean jar
  3. weigh the carrots
  4. put the tap water in the jar until you submerge completely the carrots
  5. add 2,5% of total weight (carrots+water) in salt to the jar
  6. put a weight over the carrots to sink them
  7. let them ferment in peace for at least 21 days with the jar lid CLOSED!!!
  8. after 21 days, open and enjoy.

Final considerations

If you intend not to eat all the carrots at once, keep the jar refrigerated after you open it. Better to eat all the jar in a week or so
