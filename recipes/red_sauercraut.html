Red sauercraut

how to lacto ferment delicious red sauercraut easily at home

Ingredients

  • Fresh red cabbage
  • Table salt

Steps

  1. wash the red cabbage under tap water
  2. cut the red cabbage and put them in a clean container
  3. weigh the red cabbage
  4. sprinkle 2,5% of total weight in salt over the red cabbage
  5. give them a mix until they become wet and put tem in a clean jar
  6. put a weight over the red cabbage to sink them
  7. let them ferment in peace for at least 21 days with the jar lid CLOSED!!!
  8. after 21 days, open and enjoy.

Final considerations

If you intend not to eat all the red sauercraut at once, keep the jar refrigerated after you open it. Better to eat all the jar in a week or so
